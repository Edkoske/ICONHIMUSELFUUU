HOW TO FIND NEW PEXELS IMAGES:
===============================

1. Visit: https://www.pexels.com
2. Search for keywords (e.g., "business", "technology", "teamwork")
3. Right-click the image and copy direct link
4. Replace the URL in HTML with your new image URL

IMAGE URL FORMAT (from Pexels):
https://images.pexels.com/photos/{ID}/pexels-photo-{ID}.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=400&h=300&fit=crop
